Shakshuka

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • milk
  • parsley
  • olive oil

Description

This delicious Middle Eastern dish blends a light weight meal and that spicy tomato taste. Shakshouka is an over millenia old Lebanese recipe. Pair it with some nice white wine, and you a blah blah blah.

Directions

  1. Mix spices in tomato suace
  2. pour eggs into frying pan
  3. cook eggs over low heat
